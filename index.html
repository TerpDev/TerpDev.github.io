Quantum Rush:

Quantum Rush is a mobile game I worked on with two fellow students during my second year. It's a cool endless runner game where the goal is to grab as many coins as you can. There are also power-ups scattered around that help you out. The catch? Watch out for obstacles blocking your way! You can switch lanes by swiping your finger across the screen.

The game is designed to be a fun challenge, testing your skills as you dodge obstacles and collect coins. It's a bit like a race where you want to beat your own high score. And hey, the more power-ups you grab, the better your chances. We wanted the controls to be easy, so we went for a simple swipe-your-finger mechanic to switch lanes. It's all about making the game accessible and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of gaming experience.

Working on Quantum Rush has been a collaborative effort, and it's not just about creating a game but also about learning together and having fun with the process. It's a project that combines different skills and ideas to bring something cool to life.

In a nutshell, Quantum Rush is a fun game that reflects our journey as students exploring game development in a hands-on way.

SpaceShooter:

Jump into the action of my homemade space game built with MonoGame! Your main goal is simple: rack up the highest score possible. To navigate your spacecraft, just use the W, A, S, and D keys. Your mission is to dodge incoming enemy spaceships as they descend from above. If you get hit, it's game over.

Control your spaceship with these keys and let loose a barrage of laser fire by hitting the spacebar. Each successful dodge and hit adds points to your score. But be warned, the longer you last, the tougher it gets.

Immerse yourself in the game with cool sounds and lasers lighting up the screen. This game is all my creation, made from scratch. Get ready for an exciting journey where mastering movement and precise shooting is the key to conquering the vastness of space.
